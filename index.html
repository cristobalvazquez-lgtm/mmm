Where do I start? With reservations we were given an outside table (great) with water views that were stunning! However, the music is so L O U D that a normal conversation is impossible. Then, they start (twice during our 1.5 hour long dinner) sending around a band with members places unwanted hats on guests. We ordered two appetizers (only one arrived); one of us ordered wine and a cocktail. The cocktail arrived in a timely manner but the wine only arrived AFTER the appetizers and our second request. The steak for both of us were cooked to perfection! The sides NEVER arrived -- we ordered one baked potato and one order of mashed potatoes. Despite being assured that both would be sent out asap. Our dinner consisted of an appetizer and steak. We returned to the Kempinski across the street and ordered a full proper meal for 1/2 the price. The dinner bill was over $200. Tip not included nor given. When the "manager" came to our table at the end he expressed his apologies, ok fine, but wanted to know what they could do better in the future! I'm not interested in teaching him how to do his or his staff's job. Try to stay away from this place unless you want loud music, lousy service and a bill that is outrageous! There are better places to visit in Cancun. We especially enjoyed two dinners at the Puerto Madero Restaurant nearby.

💡 Recomendaciones Estratégicas

Para Porfirio's Canún: Control de Ruido y Ambiente

🔊 Implementar sistemas de gestión de volumen durante presentaciones de mariachis. Múltiples reseñas señalan que el ruido impide conversaciones normales. Evaluar horarios de shows para balancear entretenimiento con comodidad del cliente.

Para Porfirio's Canún: Optimización de Servicio de Acompañamientos

🍽️ Estandarizar la cantidad de tortillas según número de comensales. Documentar el problema reportado sobre insuficiencia de tortillas y falta de entrega de acompañamientos (papas al horno, puré). Crear checklist operacional para completeness de órdenes.

Para Porfirio's Canún: Gestión de Expectativas de Precios

💰 Revisar estrategia de precios vs percepción de valor. Una reseña menciona que una comida completa en hotel competidor costó la mitad. Comunicar mejor el value proposition (ubicación, vista, entretenimiento) para justificar tarifa premium.

Para Harry's Cancún: Mantener Estándares de Presentación

✨ Preservar la excelencia en plating sin sacrificar porción visual. Una crítica menor señala que la carne se veía "sola" en platos grandes. Considerar ajustar tamaño de platos o decoración para mejorar percepción visual mientras mantienes generosidad de raciones.

Para Harry's Cancún: Programa de Reconocimiento a Personal

👑 Formalizar programa de incentivos para staff. Múltiples reseñas destacan meseros específicos (Jerry, Sergio, Franco, Javi, Santi, etc.). Crear sistema que reconozca y recompense consistencia en excelencia de servicio para retener talento estrella.
